PyData

PyData is an educational program of NumFOCUS, a non-profit organization in the United States. PyData provides a forum for the international community of users and developers of data analysis tools to share ideas and learn from each other. The Workshop was Organised on 9th September in which Raj Rohit Jalem, Sr. Data Scientist, Episource LLC, Conducted the “Getting into the world of Data Science” Workshop,an interactive 2-Session Hands-on workshop, which dealt with the basics of Data Science ,Its Importance And Future Scope of Data Science,using various libraries in Jupyter Notebook and Titanic Dataset CSV.It helps students to get introduce with the taste of Python and Machine Learning and alsothe skillset required in todays market.

Industry Academia Meet

IIIT, Naya Raipur is organized its first Industry-Academia Meet on October 6-7, 2017 to enable its students to interact with industry personnel and equip themselves with essential skills required for industry readiness.The main focus was to develop industry-academia linkages between our college and interested industry partners.The key objective of the event was to bring the industry and academia on the same platform to discuss issues related to industrial expectations from the institute, showcase our student technology projects,present innovations by faculty members, solve a few industrial problems in the region and explore the possibility of setting up industry sponsored labs in the institute. It Involves Technology showcasing by students Structural plan of industry interaction, Panel discussions.

Arogyaswami J. Paulraj AVSM

Professor Arogyaswami Joseph Paulraj, Stanford University, a Padma Bhushan Awardee and prestigious 2014 Marconi Society Prize His idea for using multiple antennas at both the transmitting and receiving stations – which is at the heart of the current high speed WiFi and 4G mobile systems. Padma Bhushan Dr. Arogyaswami Paulraj delivered a talk on " Evolution of Mobile Air Interface Technology" on 5th-6th March,2017. The talk mainly dealt with the MIMO concept, which has revolutionised high speed wireless delivery of multimedia services for billions of people across the globe. The Interactive Session was followed by a Panel discussion on "Future of IT and how India can Contribute Effectively" which mainly focussed on the wide scope of “How India can do better in technology innovations”.

IEEE Skill and Knowledge Enhancement Programme

The IEEE SKEP was held on 22nd-23rd October in which researchers,entrepreneurs(in IEEE Bombay Section) from across the country visited IIIIT Naya Raipur and presented on the various emerging technologies. The IEEE Bombay Section comprises of a diverse group of industry, academic and research professionals and students, with a common interest in advances in technology and leveraging them for betterment of humanity. The section has conceived the Skill and Knowledge Enhancement Program (SKEP) during the year 2013. SKEP proposes talks, discussions and distinguished lectures with an intention to reach out to industry professionals, academia and society in general.The event also had Hardware Design Contest and Poster Presentation Contest for participants which had IoT and Smart Cities as theme.

Workshop on High Performance Computing

One-Day workshop on High Performance Computing was held on 28th August,2016 at IIIT Naya Raipur.The workshop was delivered by Dr. V. C. V. Rao.He is an Associate Director in High Performance Computing - Technologies (HPC-Tech) Group, at the Centre for Development of Advanced Computing (C-DAC), Pune, India.His group at C-DAC is involved in "Development of Application and System Benchmarks for HPC Systems” as well as “Model and Development of Large Scale Application Kernels in Distributed Computing Environment (Parallel Processing Platforms with Different Accelerators (Co Processors and GPUs) and BIG Data Processing Environment (MPI & Hadoop MapReduce)”.The First session was on “Introduction to Parallel Computing – A Hands on Approach on Multi-to-Many Core Processor Systems”.The second session was on “An Overview of National Supercomputing Mission: Challenges and Efforts”.The third session was the most exciting session of the event: “Hands-on parallel programming on C-DAC’s system remotely”.
