Achievements

HULT PRIZE

The students of Dr S.P. Mukherjee IIIT Naya Raipur brought laurels to the institute by qualifying for the Hult Prize Regional Finals which would be held in Toronto, Canada in the month of March 2018. The team members from IIIT Naya Raipur include Anand Vardhan Singh, Prashant Dhillon, Siddhant Dash and Kaustubh Vats along with Hult Prize On Campus Representative, Prakshaal Jain. The team was selected from a record breaking pool of over 100,000+ applications received from over 120 countries to participate in this game changing initiative.The Hult Prize is a partnership between Hult International Business School and the Clinton Global Initiative. Former U.S. President Bill Clinton along with the United Nations Organisation, selects the challenge topic and announces the winner each September. It is named one of the top five ideas changing the world by TIME Magazine. The innovative crowdsourcing platform identifies and launches disruptive and catalytic social ventures that aim to solve the planet’s most pressing challenges. This year, the Hult Prize is focused on finding solutions by "Harnessing the Power of Energy" to transform the lives of 10 million people by 2025.

About Us

Dr. SPM IIIT-Naya Raipur, established by the International Institute of Information Technology University Act, 2013 of the Government of Chhattisgarh, is a joint venture by Chhattisgarh State Government and National Thermal Power Corporation (NTPC).The institute is committed to pursue excellence in higher education, research and development in Information Technology (IT) and associated disciplines.
